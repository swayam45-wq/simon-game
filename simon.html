How to play

Press any key on your keyboard to begin. The game will display a color sequence by flashing one of the buttons. Click the buttons in the same order as they flashed. If you get the order right, you move to the next level. Each level increases the length of the sequence. If you click the wrong button, the game ends. Your final score is the highest level you reached.
